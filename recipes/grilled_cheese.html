Grilled Cheese

Description

The grilled cheese sandwich consists of melted cheese between two grilled pieces of white bread. It is best consumed with tomato soup.

Ingredients

  • Brioche bread
  • Sliced cheese
  • Butter or Mayo
  • (Optional) Grilled turkey breast slices

Steps

  1. Place two slices of cheese between two slices of brioche bread. Add turkey breast slices if desired.
  2. On the external sides of the sandwich, apply mayo or butter.
  3. Heat up a pan on medium heat and add a tablespoon of butter, fry one side of the sandwich until golden brown.
  4. Flip and fry the other side until it turns golden brown as well.
  5. Voila! Your grilled cheese sandwich is ready. Enjoy!
