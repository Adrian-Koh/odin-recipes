Chicken Rice

Description

Hainanese Chicken Rice is a popular dish in Malaysia and Singapore. It is a healthy dish that consists of juicy boiled chicken, fragrant rice and soup.

Ingredients

  • Ginger (5 slices)
  • Scallion (2 stalks)
  • Garlic (1 bulb)
  • Rice (450g)
  • Skinless boneless chicken thighs (1kg)
  • Salt
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil

Steps

  1. Lay the chicken thighs on a plate and season both sides of each piece of chicken with salt.
  2. Boil 2 liters of water in a pot.
  3. While waiting for the water to boil, peel and crush the garlic petals. Cut and separate the green and white parts of the scallion. We are only using the green parts of the scallion for this recipe.
  4. As soon as the water starts boilng, add the ginger, scallion and garlic into the pot. Slowly place the chicken pieces into the water.
  5. Turn the heat down to low.
  6. After 20 minutes, turn off the heat. Use 500ml of the chicken broth to make rice in the rice cooker.
  7. Mix one teaspoon of sesame oil and one teaspoon of soy sauce with two tablespoons of the chicken broth. Pour it over the sliced cooked chicken thighs.
  8. Serve with rice and enjoy!
