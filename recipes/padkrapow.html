Pad Kra Pow

Description

Pad Kra Pow is a popular dish in Thailand in which pork is stir-fried with garlic, basil and chilli peppers, and served with rice and fried egg.

Ingredients (serves 2)

  • 300g uncooked rice
  • 4 eggs
  • Vegetable oil
  • Thai chilli peppers
  • Garlic
  • Thai basil
  • 300g ground pork
  • Fish sauce
  • Light soy sauce
  • Dark soy sauce
  • Oyster sauce
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Wash the rice and cook it in a rice cooker with 330ml of water.
  2. Using a pestle and mortar, grind 5 Thai chilli peppers and 5 garlic petals.
  3. Mix 2 tbsp fish sauce, 1 tbsp light soy sauce, 1 tbsp dark soy sauce, 1 tbsp oyster sauce and 1 tsp sugar in a bowl.
  4. Heat 10 grams of vegetable oil in a wok. Add the ground pork into the wok.
  5. Do not agitate the pork in the wok. Once the bottom is browned, flip to the other side until it browns.
  6. Push some of the pork to the side and place the garlic and chilli mixture in the empty space.
  7. After a minute, break up the pork and mix it up with the garlic and chilli.
  8. Once the pork is cooked, add the sauce mixture and thai basil leaves. Turn off the heat.
  9. After plating the pork, fry an egg in the same wok.
  10. Serve the pork with rice and the fried egg. Enjoy!
